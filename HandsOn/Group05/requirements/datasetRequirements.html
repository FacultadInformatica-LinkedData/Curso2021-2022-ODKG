Agenda de actividades y eventos

Group02: Aïcha Cheridi, Alexandre Morel, Adrien Simard 

R1. Data are in the smart cities domain.
This dataset is about activities of different types that are going to be held or that are being held, in municipal centers or associated with the activity managed by the City Council. You can find the activities of Cultural Centers, Sociocultural, Youth Centers, Libraries, Senior Centers, Museums, District Boards, etc.

R3.  Data have an open license so they can be published.

For the dataset about events, the general conditions allow the reuse of documents for commercial and non-commercial purposes. Reuse means the use of documents held by the Madrid City Council, provided that such use does not constitute a public administrative activity.

For the two datasets about public transport, the general conditions allow the reuse of documents for commercial and non-commercial purposes. Authorized reuse includes, by way of illustration, the activities that copy it, disseminate, modify, adapt, extract, reorganize and combine information.

R4. Data can be easily linked with generic real-world entities (e.g., locations).

There are several ways to link the data with generic real-world entities. For example with these attributes : Distrito, Barrio, Código Postal, Latitud, Longitud…

Yes, documentation exists but only for the first one : https://datos.madrid.es/FWProjects/egob/Catalogo/XComun/Ficheros/Estructura_DS_eventos_wwwmadrid.pdf
