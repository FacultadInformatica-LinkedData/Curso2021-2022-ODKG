Resource naming strategy examples

Due to the fact that our data concerns only one topic (e-scooter trips) and is small in itself, we will use a hash (#) to access the schema (classes and properties). In the case of the instancies or data resources, all of them will be accessed by the prefix /resources/*, and then, depending on the class, an aditional path will be used (trip, community, centroid or location). For this last case, a slash (/) will be used to separate the resources instead of a hash (#). The URI domain is http://e-scooter.com because it better reflects the theme of our application.

The ontological paths defined for our application are the next ones:

Schema

  • Class: http://e-scooter.com/ontology#{className}
  • Property: http://e-scooter.com/ontology#{propertyName}

Individuals

  • Trip: http://e-scooter.com/resource/trip/{id}
  • Community: http://e-scooter.com/resource/community/{AreaNumber}
  • Centroid: http://e-scooter.com/resource/centroid/CENT_{Latitude}_{Longitude}
  • Location: http://e-scooter.com/resource/location/{Latitude}_{Longitude}
