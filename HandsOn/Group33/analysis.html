Resource naming strategy examples:

    Due to the fact that our data concerns only one topic (e-scooter) and is small in itself, we will use a hash (#) to access resources. The URI domain is http://e-scooter.com because it better reflects the theme of our application.

    Ontological path:

  • Class: http://e-scooter.com/ontology#{className}
  • Property: http://e-scooter.com/ontology#{propertyName}
  • Individuals:
    • Trip: http://e-scooter.com/resource/trip/{id}
    • Community: http://e-scooter.com/resource/community/{AreaNumber}
    • Centroid: http://e-scooter.com/resource/centroid/CENT_{CentroidLatitude}%{CentroidLongitude}
    • Location: http://e-scooter.com/resource/location/{CentroidLatitude}_{CentroidLongitude}
