Available Scooter Locator

This application allows you to locate where are
the closest available scooter to you in the city of chicago
based on the information of the dataset of 2019

Write down your location

Description

On this map you will see your location and
the scooters available that are close to you

Here the user will introduce his location
so that the application can find out where
is the closest available scooter

Since the dataset has the information of
the trips made on 2019 of electric scooters
that is, the location and time when they were taken and left,
it is possible to identify what is the closest scooter available

As side objectives, would be interesting to deploy an analysis regarding
what are the most common routes and the locations that could be
added to the available area
